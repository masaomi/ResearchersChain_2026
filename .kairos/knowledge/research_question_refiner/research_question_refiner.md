# Research Question Refiner

## Purpose
Transform broad research interests into specific, testable, and answerable questions.

## PICO/PICOT Framework
- **P**opulation: Who/what is being studied?
- **I**ntervention/Exposure: What is being tested or observed?
- **C**omparison: What is the control or reference?
- **O**utcome: What is being measured?
- **T**imeframe: Over what period? (optional)

## Refinement Checklist
- [ ] Is it specific enough to be answerable with available data/methods?
- [ ] Is there a clear null hypothesis?
- [ ] Is the outcome measurable and well-defined?
- [ ] Is the scope feasible (time, budget, data availability)?
- [ ] Does it address a genuine gap in knowledge?
- [ ] Is it falsifiable?

## From Vague to Specific

| Vague | Refined |
|-------|---------|
| "How does gene X affect cancer?" | "Does overexpression of gene X increase proliferation rate in HeLa cells compared to empty vector controls over 72 hours?" |
| "ML can predict patient outcomes" | "Does a random forest model trained on clinical features achieve AUROC > 0.8 for 30-day readmission prediction in the MIMIC-IV cohort?" |
