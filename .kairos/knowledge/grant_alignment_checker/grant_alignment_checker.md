# Grant Alignment Checker

## Purpose
Ensure research proposals align with funding agency requirements and evaluation criteria.

## Alignment Checklist

### Scope Fit
- [ ] Does the topic match the call's priority areas?
- [ ] Is the methodology within the funder's scope?
- [ ] Does the team composition meet requirements?
- [ ] Is the budget within the allowed range?

### Evaluation Criteria Mapping
Map your proposal to the funder's stated criteria:

| Criterion (example) | Where Addressed | Strength |
|---------------------|----------------|----------|
| Scientific excellence | Aims, Methods | Strong/Moderate/Weak |
| Innovation | Background, Approach | Strong/Moderate/Weak |
| Feasibility | Timeline, Team, Preliminary data | Strong/Moderate/Weak |
| Impact | Significance, Dissemination plan | Strong/Moderate/Weak |
| Ethics | Ethics section | Complete/Incomplete |

### Common Weaknesses
- Aims too ambitious for the timeline/budget
- Innovation claim not supported by literature gap
- Missing preliminary data
- Dissemination plan too vague
- Budget not justified line by line

### Pre-Submission Review
- [ ] All required sections present
- [ ] Page/word limits respected
- [ ] References formatted per guidelines
- [ ] All co-PI signatures and letters obtained
- [ ] Institutional approval completed
