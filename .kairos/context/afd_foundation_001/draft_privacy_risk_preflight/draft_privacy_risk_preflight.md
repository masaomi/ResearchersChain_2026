# Privacy Risk Preflight (Draft)
## Checklist
1. Is informed consent scope sufficient for this analysis?
2. Is data de-identified or anonymized?
3. Are there re-identification risks (rare variants, small populations)?
4. Is data governance compliant (GDPR, HIPAA, institutional policy)?
5. Are data transfer agreements in place (if cross-institutional)?
6. Output: risk assessment with mitigation recommendations
